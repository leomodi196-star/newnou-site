Care

Good to Know

How should I care for NEWNOU jewelry?

Keep pieces dry when possible, wipe gently after wear, and store them separately in a soft pouch.

Are the pieces adjustable?

Many necklaces and bracelets include extender chains, and selected rings are designed with flexible sizing.

Is NEWNOU good for gifting?

Yes. The collection focuses on easy-to-style silhouettes, accessible pricing, and pieces that work across occasions.
